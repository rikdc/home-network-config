Thank you for installing the Frigate template!

1. External Secret Required:
   Create a Kubernetes secret with the following keys:
   - mqtt-user
   - mqtt-password
   - rtsp-password

2. Configuration:
   - Replace camera stream URLs in the configMap
   - Adjust resource limits based on your hardware
   - Update MQTT and camera settings in values.yaml

3. Access the UI:
   Open http://{{ .Values.ingress.host }} in your browser

4. Verify the installation:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.namespace }}

For more information, visit: https://docs.frigate.video/
